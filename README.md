# AI Virtual Staging & Smart Interior Toolkit

AI Virtual Staging & Smart Interior Toolkit is an open-source collection of prompts, workflows, and templates for AI-powered real estate virtual staging, renovation visualization, and smart interior concept design.

This project helps real estate agents, interior designers, renovation companies, smart home builders, and AI creators use generative AI more effectively without requiring advanced prompt engineering knowledge.

---

## Overview

Real estate agents, interior designers, renovation companies, and smart home professionals often need to show the potential of a space before it is renovated, staged, furnished, or redesigned.

However, many non-technical users do not know how to write effective prompts for AI image generation, interior visualization, or design proposal workflows.

This project provides practical, reusable, and easy-to-understand prompt templates and workflows so that professionals can create better AI-powered visual concepts for real estate, renovation, and smart interior design.

## Why This Project Matters

AI image generation and visual design tools are becoming increasingly useful for real estate, renovation, and interior design.

However, the quality of AI-generated results depends heavily on how clearly the user describes the goal, space, style, constraints, and output requirements.

This toolkit helps users create more consistent and practical results by providing structured prompts and workflows for real-world use cases.

The goal is not only to generate beautiful images, but also to help users communicate the potential of a space more clearly.

## Example Prompt: Empty Room Conversion

```text
Transform this cluttered apartment room into a clean, empty, real-estate-ready space.

Keep the original room structure, windows, flooring, wall layout, natural lighting, ceiling height, and door positions.

Remove furniture, boxes, personal items, cables, trash, and visual clutter.

The final image should look realistic, bright, clean, and suitable for a professional real estate listing.

Do not change the architectural layout.
Do not alter the window size or room proportions.
Do not add unrealistic furniture.
```

### Korean Explanation

이 프롬프트는 짐이 많은 부동산 매물 사진을 깨끗한 공실 이미지로 바꾸기 위한 용도입니다.

핵심은 방 구조, 창문, 바닥, 벽 배치, 자연광은 유지하면서 가구, 박스, 개인 물건, 전선, 잡동사니만 제거하는 것입니다.

## Example Prompt: Smart Interior Lighting

```text
Create a premium smart interior lighting concept for this room.

Design the lighting with warm indirect lighting, comfortable brightness, no harsh glare, smart ambient lighting, hidden LED strips, elegant ceiling and wall lighting, and a relaxing high-end residential atmosphere.

The space should feel modern, calm, comfortable, and luxurious.

Do not make the lighting look like a nightclub.
Do not overuse strong neon colors.
Keep the design realistic and suitable for a premium apartment renovation.
```

### Korean Explanation

이 프롬프트는 고급 아파트 리노베이션에 어울리는 스마트 조명 콘셉트를 만들기 위한 용도입니다.

강한 네온 조명보다 은은한 간접조명, 편안한 밝기, 고급스러운 분위기를 중심으로 합니다.

## Workflow Example

A basic real estate virtual staging workflow:

```text
1. Analyze the original property photo.
2. Identify the room type, lighting condition, clutter level, and architectural structure.
3. Choose the output goal: empty room conversion, modern staging, luxury staging, or smart interior concept.
4. Apply the appropriate prompt template.
5. Review the result for realism, structure preservation, and listing suitability.
6. Create real estate listing copy, SNS captions, or renovation proposal content.
```
