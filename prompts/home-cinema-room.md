# Home Cinema Room Prompt

## Purpose

This prompt creates a realistic home cinema concept for smart interior and entertainment-focused renovation.

## Prompt

```text
Create a premium home cinema room concept for a residential interior.

Use controlled cinematic ambient lighting, comfortable seating, subtle smart lighting, dark but warm materials, and an immersive entertainment mood.

Keep the space realistic and suitable for a real apartment or house.

Avoid excessive futuristic design, nightclub lighting, and unrealistic screen proportions.
```
