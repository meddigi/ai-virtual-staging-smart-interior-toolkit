# Empty Room Conversion Prompt

## Purpose

This prompt helps convert cluttered real estate photos into clean, empty-room visuals for property listings.

이 프롬프트는 짐이 많은 부동산 매물 사진을 깨끗한 공실 이미지로 바꾸기 위한 용도입니다.

---

## Prompt

```text
Transform this cluttered apartment room into a clean, empty, real-estate-ready space.

Keep:
- Original room structure
- Windows
- Flooring
- Wall layout
- Natural lighting
- Ceiling height
- Door positions

Remove:
- Furniture
- Boxes
- Personal items
- Cables
- Trash
- Visual clutter

The final image should look realistic, bright, clean, and suitable for a professional real estate listing.

Do not change the architectural layout.
Do not add unrealistic furniture.
Do not alter the window size or room proportions.
```

---

## Korean Explanation

방 구조는 유지하고, 가구와 잡동사니만 제거하는 것이 핵심입니다. 부동산 매물 사진처럼 밝고 깨끗하며 현실적인 결과물을 목표로 합니다.
