# Luxury Living Room Prompt

## Purpose

This prompt creates a premium living room concept for real estate marketing or renovation proposals.

## Prompt

```text
Create a realistic luxury living room interior concept.

Use a warm modern premium apartment style with balanced furniture placement, elegant materials, soft indirect lighting, and professional real estate photography quality.

Keep the original room structure, window positions, ceiling height, floor layout, and wall proportions.

The result should feel calm, high-end, comfortable, and suitable for a premium residential renovation proposal.
```
