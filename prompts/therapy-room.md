# Therapy Room Prompt

## Purpose

This prompt creates a calming therapy room concept focused on relaxation, focus, comfort, and recovery mood.

## Prompt

```text
Create a calming therapy room concept for a residential smart interior.

Use soft indirect lighting, warm neutral colors, natural textures, peaceful composition, and a comfortable recovery-focused atmosphere.

The space should feel restful, quiet, clean, and emotionally comfortable.

Avoid medical claims. Focus on relaxation, comfort, focus, and recovery mood.
```
