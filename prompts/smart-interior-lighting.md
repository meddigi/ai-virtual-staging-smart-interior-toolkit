# Smart Interior Lighting Prompt

## Purpose

This prompt helps generate premium smart lighting concepts for interior design and smart home visualization.

이 프롬프트는 고급 스마트 인테리어 조명 콘셉트를 만들기 위한 용도입니다.

---

## Prompt

```text
Create a premium smart interior lighting concept for this room.

Design the lighting with:
- Warm indirect lighting
- Comfortable brightness
- No harsh glare
- Smart ambient lighting
- Hidden LED strips
- Elegant ceiling and wall lighting
- A relaxing high-end residential atmosphere

The space should feel modern, calm, comfortable, and luxurious.

Do not make the lighting look like a nightclub.
Do not overuse strong neon colors.
Keep the design realistic and suitable for a premium apartment renovation.
```

---

## Korean Explanation

강한 네온 조명보다 은은한 간접조명, 편안한 밝기, 눈부심 없는 조명, 고급 아파트 리노베이션에 어울리는 분위기를 중심으로 합니다.
